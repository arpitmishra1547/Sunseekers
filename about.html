About SunSeekers

Project Overview

SunSeekers is an advanced dual-axis solar tracking system designed to maximize solar energy collection efficiency. Our system uses cutting-edge technology to automatically adjust solar panels to follow the sun's movement throughout the day.

Key Features

Dual-Axis Tracking

Precise movement in both horizontal and vertical axes for optimal sun alignment

Smart Sensors

Advanced LDR sensors for accurate light detection and positioning

Real-time Monitoring

Live tracking and visualization of system performance

Technology Stack

Hardware

  • Arduino ESP32 Microcontroller
  • Dual Servo Motors
  • LDR Light Sensors
  • Solar Panel Assembly

Software

  • Arduino IDE
  • HTML5 & CSS3
  • JavaScript
  • Chart.js for Visualization
